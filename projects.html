Featured Project

Paint It Pink

St. Petersburg, FL Community Mural Event

An entire downtown St. Petersburg block — six historic buildings at 4th Ave. S and 4th St. S — was painted pink as a bold farewell before demolition to make way for the Roche Bobois St. Pete Tower, a 29-story art-infused luxury condo development by Valor Real Estate.

The vibrant pink canvas became the stage for a mural flash mob featuring dozens of local artists who transformed every wall, door, and surface into original works of art. The project celebrated St. Petersburg's identity as an arts community and proved that temporary public art can leave a lasting impact.

"Public art should bring people together, and even if it's for a short period of time, it can have so many positive effects on a community." — Helen French, St. Petersburg Arts Alliance

Photography: Becca McCoy / Leafmore Studios

Organized by: Vitale Bros & Valor Real Estate Development

Featured Project

Hot Walls

Gulfport, FL — Overspray Art Space Street Art & Skate Culture

An epic night of street art and skate culture at Overspray Art Space featuring new murals by 20+ Tampa Bay artists. The event lives by its tagline: "Create. Destroy. Repeat." — knowing these works will eventually be painted over to make room for new expressions.

The inaugural event on July 12, 2025 featured live DJs, a Tiki bar, an indoor "Cold Walls" gallery with prints and merch from the Bay Area Street Collective, and a fiery finale — a skateboarding stunt show by Anchor Skate Supply with tricks on a flaming rail and through a ring of fire.

"This show feels like the old days in a good way. Very pure, pure-hearted." — Bask (Artist)

Photography: Becca McCoy / Leafmore Studios

Organized by: The Vitale Brothers

Notable Artists: Zulu Painter, Derek Donnelly, Bask, Palehorse, Freeman, Jujmo, Sionna, Chris Dyer & more
