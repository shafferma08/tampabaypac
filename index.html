Our Story

We Empower Public Artists

Public Art Collective (PAC) is a nonprofit founded by working artists to advocate, organize, and fund public art across Tampa Bay. We believe artists deserve fair compensation, creative freedom, and real opportunities.

Whether you're a muralist, street artist, or installation creator, PAC exists to connect you with projects that map the cultural DNA of our cities. We handle grants, partnerships, and logistics so you can stay focused on creating.

PAC is part of an ecosystem built by the same team of Tampa Bay artists. Each branch serves a different role — together, they create a full support system for working artists.

Overspray Art Space

The community studio where artists train, practice, and connect. Open workshops and spray paint sessions for all skill levels.

Vitale Bros

The professional mural company that brings in commercial projects — and hires PAC roster artists to paint them.

Public Art Collective

The nonprofit that ties it all together — securing grants, advocating for fair pay, and connecting artists to every opportunity.
